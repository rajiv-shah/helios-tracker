How Sun Wavelengths Penetrate Your Body

"Longer wavelengths of sunlight penetrate through the human body and have the ability to improve physiological function systemically, even without eye involvement."

Thoracic Penetration

Near-infrared wavelengths from sunlight (specifically 830–860 nm) pass completely through the human chest/thorax. While clothing reduces intensity slightly, it does not stop this absorption.

Mitochondrial Stimulation

When absorbed, these wavelengths excite Cytochrome C Oxidase inside cells, sparking a massive upgrade in mitochondrial ATP (cellular fuel) production.

Systemic Distal Impact

Lab subjects exposed to 15-minute thoracic NIR sessions showed significantly improved vision and cognitive alertness 24 hours later, proving localized light exposure travels systemically.

About Helios Tracker

Version & Guide

HELIOS TRACKER

v1.2.0 Official Release

Helios is a Progressive Web Application designed for iOS Safari Standalone to monitor and log your daily exposure to therapeutic solar light wavelengths. It models UV-triggered Vitamin D3 synthesis and clothes-transmitting Near-Infrared (NIR) deep tissue charging.

☀️ Simple User Guide

1
Configure Skin & Clothes

Set your Fitzpatrick Skin Type and Clothing Level. These variables calibrate the Vitamin D rate and UV limits (sunburn protection thresholds).

2
Plan Around Windows

Check the active therapeutic window (Morning NIR Prime, Midday Vitamin D, or Evening NIR Recovery) to optimize your outdoor timing.

3
Start Outdoors

Click "Start Solar Session" when stepping outside. Place the app in your pocket or hold it; NIR is computed for both exposed and covered chest areas.

4
Save Your Session

Watch your live doses rise and seek shade if sunburn alarms trigger. Tap End & Save Session to log your minutes, Vitamin D, and NIR totals into memory and update your weekly charts.

5
Backup & Import Backups

Prevent data loss! Scroll to the Weekly Progress panel and tap Backup Data to download your history as a `.json` file, or select Restore Backup to import and merge your saved logs.

💡 PWA Standalone Features

  • Offline Access: Fully cached service worker lets you launch offline.
  • Add to Home Screen: Tap Share and select "Add to Home Screen" to open as a full-screen, standalone app.

Add Helios to Home Screen

To experience Helios as a standalone iPhone app, install it directly onto your Home Screen:

  1. Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari.
  2. Scroll down and select Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add in the top-right corner to complete.
